Help

Hi there! Welcome to PoE Atlas.

You can navigate this site similarly to the Atlas within PoE. (Zoom, pan, hover over maps, etc.)

However, this site also has some unique features that the in-game atlas does not have:

Improved Search: In addition to searching for specific maps, you can also search by map tier.

Clickable Maps: You can click any map node to bring up an information pane which shows the tiers at which that map can drop, as well as a list of all the map nodes it connects to at the region's highest watchstone level.
